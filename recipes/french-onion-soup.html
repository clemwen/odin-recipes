French Onion Soup

Description

This soup is pretty good. Very simple, but just as decadent. It is somewhat labor intensive, but after your

shoulder and eyes ache from cutting a large batch of onions, this soup will heal you.

Ingredients

  • Yellow Onions, 3lb
  • Butter, 2tbsp
  • Leek, 1
  • Shallot, 1
  • Beef stock, like a cup or 2
  • White wine, 1/2 cup
  • French Bread, 1 loaf
  • White cheese, enough to cover bread

Steps

  1. Chop leeks, onions, and shallots into ~1/4" half-rings.
  2. On medium heat, add butter and l/o/s mix and stir regularly. ~30-45 min.
  3. Once onions are translucent and uniformly brown, add wine and stir constantly to deglaze pan. Cook off liquid.
  4. Add beef stock and let simmer on low until serving.
  5. Place 1" slices of bread on to sheet pan and add cheese, covering entire surface. Place in broiler for 1-2 min. Keep an eye on it.
  6. Place bread into wide bowl and ladle onion soup on top.
