Grilled Peaches and Sherry a la Mode

Description

Peaches and cream.

Ingredients

  • Peaches
  • Vanilla ice cream
  • Sherry
  • Fresh mint

Steps

  1. Grill peaches until soft, ~30 min
  2. Cut into wedges and place next to a scoop of vanilla ice cream.
  3. Pour a splash of sherry on top.
  4. Add a few mint leaves on top.got
