Apizza!

Description

This pizza dough is thin and crispy. Firm and easy to work with and maintains integrity when sliced up.

Ingredients

  • High gluten bread flour
  • Whole wheat flour
  • Brown sugar
  • EVOO
  • Extra fine sea salt
  • Yeast
  • Water

Steps

A stand mixer helps, but you can do this in a large mixing bowl.

  1. Measure out all ingredients in grams.
  2. Add all of yeast, and ~1/4 of bread flour to cover it up.
  3. Add ~1/4 of water and mix briefly and let sit until bubbling.
  4. Add remaining flour and water. Add oil and sugar to water to avoid clumping and continue mixing.
  5. Add salt last and continue mixing until mixture becomes uniform and all flour has been absorbed.
  6. Dough should become a singular, firm object, but not mixed much further to avoid overworking.
  7. Portion into desired weight and fold into spheres.
  8. Let proof in refrigerator for at least 48 hrs.
